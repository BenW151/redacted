About Us

[01]

Established in Bristol in 2019, REDACTED stands at the cutting edge of alternative electronic music. We champion artists who push boundaries and innovate, creating deep, dark sounds that captivate a global audience.

Form

Form explores the structure and style of our music. It reflects our artists' mastery in molding complex sonic elements into cohesive, engaging tracks. This section highlights their skill in crafting unique musical forms that defy traditional boundaries and resonate deeply with listeners.

Function

Function delves into the purpose and impact of our music. It's about creating tracks that not only pulse with life but also convey powerful emotions and messages. Our artists use their sounds as tools for expression, connecting with audiences on a visceral level and leaving a lasting impression.
